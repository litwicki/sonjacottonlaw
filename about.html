Sonja Cotton & Associates specializes in the recruitment and placement of Attorneys, Administrators, Paralegals, Secretaries, Receptionists, Legal Support Staff, Records Specialists, Librarians, Word Processors and Docket Clerks in permanent and temporary/contract positions in corporate legal departments and law firms.

With its proven track record for success, Sonja Cotton & Associates (SCA) is a leader in the industry in providing top talent for many law firms and corporate hiring departments in Arizona. Most of our professional pool has come to us on a referral basis and are confidentially searching for the career designed to suit their specific needs. Our highly-trained legal placement specialists welcome any hiring challenge and take pride in assisting our candidates and clients to find the right fit. Along with SCA’s extensive database of impressive candidates and our active referral and recruiting network, our team of specialists are able to find a solution to any legal staffing need with service that is second to none. You can trust Sonja Cotton & Associates to be your legal placement specialists.

Ms. Cotton is the founder of Sonja Cotton & Associates and has over 18 years combined legal recruiting and law firm experience in various capacities, including legal secretary, paralegal and attorney recruiting coordinator. Early in her career she served as the in-house attorney recruiter for a prominent Phoenix law firm and later gained further substantial training in human resources consulting from a top-rated executive search firm.

She has extensive contacts within the legal community and has placed hundreds of qualified candidates into rewarding new careers. Ms. Cotton specializes in the placement of attorneys and legal support staff and has earned a reputation for outstanding service and professionalism.
